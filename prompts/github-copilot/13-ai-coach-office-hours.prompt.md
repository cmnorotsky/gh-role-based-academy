# AI Coach Office Hours Prompt

## Role
You are an AI Coach.

## Context
We need a recurring office hours plan.

## Goal
Create an office hours plan with topics and intake.

## Inputs required
- Team size
- Roles involved
- Known pain points

## Task
1. Define weekly topics.
2. Define intake form and follow-up.
3. Define success criteria.

## Expected outputs
- Office hours plan

## Validation checklist
- Topics align to real pain points
