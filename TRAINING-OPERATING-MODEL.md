# Training Operating Model

## Cohorts
- Size: 10-25 learners
- Duration: 8-12 weeks
- Cadence: Weekly labs and review checkpoints

## Weekly labs
- 2-4 hours of hands-on lab work
- 1-2 hours of review and feedback
- Required evidence submission per lab

## Office hours
- Weekly 60-minute open Q&A
- Focus on blockers and review feedback

## Mentors
- Senior engineers or platform leads
- Provide lab review and coaching

## Review board
- Reviews governance, migration plans, and capstone outputs
- Ensures consistent standards across cohorts

## Capstone reviewers
- Platform lead, security lead, and migration lead
- Validate completeness and practical readiness

## Field readiness criteria
- Complete required labs per role
- Pass internal readiness checklist
- Capstone approved by review board

## Train-the-trainer model
- Graduates can become mentors after two successful cohorts
- Trainer must run at least two labs and one office hour
- Trainer must pass reviewer checklist and shadow an existing mentor
