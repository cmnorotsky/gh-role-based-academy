- Copilot and AI-SDLC modules

## Estimated time
10-12 hours

2. Client readiness assessment
3. Team shadowing and workflow discovery

5. Prompt coaching for BA/QA/Product
6. AI-assisted refactoring and testing
7. AI-assisted DevOps and infra
8. AI safety, security, review practices
9. Office hours and embedded coaching
10. AI Coach value reporting
11. Handoff to client champions
12. AI Coach capstone

## Deliverables
- AI Coach enablement plan and value report

## Review criteria
- Enablement plan is practical and safe
