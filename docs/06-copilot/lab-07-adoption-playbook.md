# Lab 07: Adoption Playbook

## Objective
Create a Copilot adoption plan.

## Target audience
Enablement leads

## Prerequisites
- Lab 06

## Estimated time
2-3 hours

## Tools required
- Adoption templates

## Step-by-step lab
1. Define pilot teams and timeline.
2. Define enablement activities.
3. Define office hours model.
4. Define success criteria.

## Common mistakes
- No follow-up plan after pilot

## Evidence to submit
- Adoption playbook

## Review checklist
- Clear timeline and owners

## Definition of done
- Adoption playbook approved
